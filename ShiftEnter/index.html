The Show

Millions of Americans have imagined themselves on the campaign trail but few know where to begin and what it really takes to run for and win public office. The Candidate provides an inside look at the challenges, excitement, frustration, power-struggles, and far-less glamorous elements of a political race and public office holding. Viewers will cheer on their favorites as candidates receive resources and mentorship needed to succeed. Ultimately, the success and failure of each candidate will be theirs and theirs alone.


We are casting women and men with little to no prior political experience. You can self-declare, if you up for the challenge, or nominate anyone and start the buzz around them. Candidates can be from both parties or no party at all, and the office sought can be at the national, state, or local level.

About Us


We are a group of students and fellows from Harvard working with media professionals and political consultants. We believe fervently in our nation's promise that "We the People" are responsible for a more perfect union. Our goal is to empower citizens to believe that they can be part of the solution, re-energize the electorate and to launch the next generation of political leaders from the grassroots.
